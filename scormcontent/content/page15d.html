How To Use This E-learning

Throughout this programme there are a number of common controls that will allow you to navigate through the pages and take part in the activities.

The Next button always moves you on to the screen. The next button is inactive when you are required to complete an activity before being able to move on.
The Back button is similar to Next but will take you back to the previous screen.
The Help button will launch a screen displaying this information on common controls.
The About button will launch a screen displaying information about this package.
The Exit button will close the programme.
Time to Care tells the story of Ella, an elderly patient, attending hospital for a routine assessment. Memorable video clips demonstrate both positive and negative experiences. Engaging interactions and high quality content designed by subject matter experts; will help your learners to provide Ella with an excellent experience of care.

All our e-learning programmes are SCORM compliant, so you can host them on your own Learning Management System or we can host them for you.

WHO IS IT FOR?

All staff who come into contact with patients, relatives and carers.
